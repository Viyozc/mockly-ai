    FAQ

    Is the audit really free?

    Yes. The on-page audit runs entirely in your browser and costs nothing. No account needed.

    Do I need to upload my images?

    No. You describe your image setup with a few clicks. Mockly scores the strategy, not the pixels — so it works before you've generated anything.

    How is the score calculated?

    We weight image count, lifestyle-vs-flat ratio, marketplace compliance (Amazon white-bg vs Etsy lifestyle), template-clone risk, and conversion readiness (shadows, lighting, resolution) — the signals that drive tap-through and add-to-cart.

    Will this guarantee more sales?

    No tool can. But strong, differentiated, compliant images are the single biggest lever on conversion — and most listings leave easy points on the table. Mockly shows you exactly where.
